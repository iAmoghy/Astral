A fun space-shooter adventure

Hunt down asteroids through belts of progressing difficulty to gather precious Cerentaium metal - vital for the extraction of Astrallite.

Expand your Mars colony

A brand-new clicker-like menu allows you to create machinery and tools using Cerentaium from your space expeditions.
Create meadows of Astrallite-blooming flowers and lakes of molten Astrallite, all at the click of a button.

Upgrade your ship to new levels

Invest some of your Astrallite to make your ship even more poweful - faster lasers, longer invincibilty, stronger shields - it's all waiting for you to purchase.
